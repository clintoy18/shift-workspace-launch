Four feature cards in a responsive grid:
- **Real-Time Availability**: Interactive visual showing available desks/zones
- **24/7 Access Control**: Digital key and security feature showcase
- **Seamless Payments**: Automated billing visualization
- **Booking Preview**: "Select Your Zone" interface (Hot Desk vs. Dedicated Office)

### 4. Member Dashboard Teaser
- Glassmorphism card with mock user profile
- Shows: User avatar, current booking time, Wi-Fi credentials
- "Unlock Your Dashboard" CTA
- Floating design over a blurred workspace image

### 5. Pricing Table
- Three tiers: Day Pass, Monthly Hot Desk, Dedicated Office
- Feature comparison list
- Most popular tier highlighted
- CTAs for each tier

### 6. Testimonials Section
- Carousel or grid of member testimonials
- Photos, names, and company names
- Star ratings

### 7. Location Section
- Embedded map or location preview
- Address and contact details

### 8. FAQ Section
- Accordion-style common questions

### 9. Lead Capture Section
